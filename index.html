About

I am an AI researcher at the National University of Singapore. My research focuses on multimodal misinformation, trustworthy machine learning, and the evaluation of large multimodal models. I am especially interested in building benchmarks and methods that improve explainability, robustness, and generalizability in real-world multimodal settings.
